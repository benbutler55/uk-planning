# First Reform Package (MVP Draft)

This first package prioritizes practical measures that can reduce delay quickly while preparing statutory changes for structural issues.

## Rapid 0-12 Month Actions

- Standardize validation requirements nationally for common application types.
- Introduce synchronized policy update windows to reduce central-local drift.
- Require measurable local-fit design checklists at submission stage.

## 12-24 Month Structural Actions

- Clarify legal precedence where local criteria conflict with strategic delivery obligations.
- Create a coordinated evidence gateway for EIA and habitats-linked requirements.

## Expected Effects

- Lower validation churn and front-end delay.
- Fewer appeal reversals caused by policy interpretation differences.
- Higher first-submission compliance and improved certainty for developers and decision-makers.
