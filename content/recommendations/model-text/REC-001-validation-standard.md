# REC-001: National Standard Validation Checklist

## Problem

Validation requirements for planning applications vary materially between LPAs. Applicants submitting to multiple authorities face different document requirements for identical development types, generating avoidable resubmissions and front-end delay. The DMPO 2015 and PPG provide a framework but permit wide local discretion in validation checklists, leading to inconsistency.

## Proposed Change

MHCLG to publish a mandatory national validation checklist for each defined application category (householder, minor, major, EIA, NSIP, prior approval) as a statutory document under the DMPO 2015. LPAs may add local requirements only where justified in writing with reference to specific adopted local plan policy.

## Model Guidance Text

> **Validation Standard Notice (model)**
>
> Under the Town and Country Planning (Development Management Procedure) (England) Order 2015 (as amended), the following documents are required for validation of a [major residential] planning application:
>
> **National mandatory requirements:**
> 1. Completed application form (1APP standard)
> 2. Ownership certificate and agricultural land declaration
> 3. Design and access statement (where required by DMPO Article 9)
> 4. Site location plan at 1:1250 or 1:2500 scale
> 5. Block plan at 1:500 scale
> 6. Existing and proposed floor plans and elevations
> 7. Fee payment at correct rate per current fee schedule
>
> **Additional local requirements (where applicable):**
> Any local validation requirement must be listed here with reference to the adopted local plan policy that justifies it. Where no local policy basis exists, the requirement may not be imposed.
>
> Applications meeting the national requirements above shall not be returned invalid solely on the basis of an unlisted local requirement.

## Implementation Notes

- Requires amendment to DMPO 2015 Schedule 1 to make national checklist statutory.
- LPAs must publish any local additions on their website with policy justification.
- MHCLG to issue updated PPG section on validation within 6 months of guidance publication.
- Transition period of 3 months for LPAs to align local checklists.

## KPI

- Target: 30 percent reduction in validation rework rate across pilot LPAs within 12 months.
- Baseline: approximately 10-15 percent of submissions returned as invalid nationally (PS2 proxy).
