# REC-005: Baseline Local-Fit Design and Transport Code

## Problem

Design and transport policy requirements are set out in local plans and SPDs with varying degrees of specificity. Applicants cannot reliably assess compliance prior to submission. Officers apply discretionary tests that are not consistently measured, leading to negotiation rather than compliance checking, wasting officer and applicant time.

## Proposed Change

LPAs to adopt a measurable local-fit baseline design and transport checklist as part of their Development Management Policies DPD or SPD, setting out quantified minimum standards (e.g. street frontage percentage, parking ratios, cycle storage, accessible unit proportion) against which compliance can be objectively assessed at validation stage.

## Model Checklist Template

> **Local-Fit Baseline Compliance Checklist (model)**
>
> The following standards apply to all major residential applications within [LPA name]. Applications must include a completed compliance statement confirming conformity or a reasoned justification for non-conformity.
>
> **Design standards:**
> - Minimum 70 percent active frontage on primary street elevations
> - Maximum plot coverage [X percent] for residential schemes
> - Minimum [X]m rear garden depth for houses
> - Landscaping provision: minimum [X] sq m per unit
>
> **Transport and accessibility:**
> - Car parking: maximum [X] spaces per dwelling type (per adopted parking standards)
> - Cycle storage: minimum 1 secure space per dwelling plus visitor provision
> - Accessible units: minimum 10 percent M4(3) wheelchair accessible dwellings; 30 percent M4(2) adaptable dwellings
>
> **Submission requirement:**
> A signed Design and Transport Compliance Statement must accompany all major applications. Non-conformity with any standard must be explained with reference to site-specific constraints or planning justification.

## Implementation Notes

- Adopted via SPD or DPD revision — does not require statutory instrument.
- Pilot in 2 LPAs (recommended: Oxford and Newham from pilot set) over 6 months.
- MHCLG to publish model template as part of Design Codes PPG update.
- Validation officers to be trained to apply checklist at receipt stage.

## KPI

- Target: 75 percent first-submission compliance rate with baseline checklist.
- Baseline: estimated 50-60 percent compliance on first submission (pilot LPA officer assessment).
