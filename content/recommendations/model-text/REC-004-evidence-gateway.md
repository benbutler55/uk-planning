# REC-004: Single Coordinated Evidence Gateway for EIA and Habitats

## Problem

Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) under SI 2017/571 and Habitats Regulations Assessment (HRA) under SI 2017/1012 require substantially overlapping ecological and environmental evidence, but are administered under separate legal regimes with separate consultation processes. Applicants must commission near-identical surveys twice, and LPAs and statutory consultees process duplicate requests. This extends pre-determination periods for affected schemes by an estimated 6-18 months.

## Proposed Change

Amend EIA Regulations 2017 and introduce a cross-regime coordination protocol to allow a single evidence gateway submission satisfying both EIA and HRA information requirements, with a single statutory consultee review process producing a combined response.

## Model Statutory Instrument Text

> **Town and Country Planning (Environmental Assessment Coordination) (England) Regulations [Year]**
>
> 3.— (1) Where a development proposal is subject to both Environmental Impact Assessment under the Town and Country Planning (Environmental Impact Assessment) Regulations 2017 and Appropriate Assessment under the Conservation of Habitats and Species Regulations 2017, the applicant may submit a Combined Environmental Evidence Report (CEER) satisfying the information requirements of both regimes in a single document.
>
> (2) A CEER submitted under paragraph (1) shall be treated as satisfying the requirements of—
> (a) Regulation 18 (environmental statement) of the EIA Regulations 2017; and
> (b) Regulation 63 (appropriate assessment) of the Habitats Regulations 2017 for the purposes of initial information gathering.
>
> (3) Where a CEER has been submitted, the relevant statutory consultees shall provide a single coordinated response within 42 days, addressing both EIA and HRA requirements, unless the applicant and authority agree an extended period in writing.
>
> (4) The Secretary of State may publish a standard CEER template and methodology guidance.

## Implementation Notes

- Requires primary legislation amendment or Henry VIII power to modify both SIs concurrently.
- Pilot in 2 LPAs over 12 months before national rollout.
- MHCLG and DESNZ joint working group to develop CEER template.

## KPI

- Target: 25 percent reduction in median determination time for EIA/habitats-affected schemes.
- Baseline: approximately 40-60 percent of major EIA applications decided within statutory period (P151).
