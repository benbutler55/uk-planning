# Pilot LPAs — Pilot Release

The Pilot Release uses six pilot authorities selected for representativeness and scalability. This set intentionally spans governance forms, growth conditions, and constraint profiles.

## Final Pilot Set

1. Manchester City Council
2. Oxford City Council
3. South Cambridgeshire District Council
4. Cornwall Council
5. Newham London Borough Council
6. Kent County Council (minerals and waste planning layer)

## Why This Set

- Governance diversity: London borough, metropolitan district, district, unitary, and county planning function.
- Market variation: very high urban growth, constrained high-demand city, growth corridor, and dispersed rural-coastal markets.
- Constraint variation: green belt, heritage, flood, transport, designated landscapes, and strategic minerals/waste issues.
- Practicality: all have accessible plan documentation suitable for repeatable ingestion workflows.

## Selection Rules for Scale-Out

When expanding to all England LPAs, each additional cohort should preserve diversity across:
- governance type,
- market context,
- constraint profile,
- data quality and document accessibility.
