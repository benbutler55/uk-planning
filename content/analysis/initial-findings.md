# Initial Findings (Pilot Release)

## Highest-Impact Frictions Identified

1. Validation inconsistency across LPAs creates avoidable rework and delays before determination starts.
2. Policy-weight ambiguity between national housing objectives and local restrictive criteria increases refusal volatility.
3. Environmental assessment sequencing can duplicate evidence burdens for complex schemes.

## Early Quantification Priorities

- Measure validation fail/rework rates for pilot LPAs.
- Segment determination time by application class and issue profile.
- Track appeal outcomes linked to policy-weight disputes.

## Immediate Research Actions

- Capture validation checklists for each pilot LPA.
- Extract recent appeal decisions citing policy conflict.
- Map environmental evidence requests by consent route.
