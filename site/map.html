Plain-language guide

This page helps you understand LPA Map without specialist planning jargon. Use the start-here notes first, then open linked detail pages for evidence and next actions.

Common terms: s106 (legal agreement on development obligations), NSIP (nationally significant infrastructure project), verification state (draft or reviewed data status).

Explore authorities geographically, compare performance at a glance, and open individual LPA profile pages from map markers.

All 34 LPAs in scope. The default view uses boundary-led choropleth cells (proxy geometry) coloured by major decision speed (green = above England average 74%, amber = 65-74%, red = below 65%). Use layer controls to toggle boundary fill and markers.
