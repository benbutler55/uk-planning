This page helps you understand Plan Hierarchy Explorer without specialist planning jargon. Use the start-here notes first, then open linked detail pages for evidence and next actions.

Common terms: s106 (legal agreement on development obligations), NSIP (nationally significant infrastructure project), verification state (draft or reviewed data status).
